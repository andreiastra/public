# Photo Competition Summary: 'My Favourite Waterbody' 2026

## Overview
The **'My Favourite Waterbody'** national photography competition is an annual event organized by the **Local Authority Waters Programme (LAWPRO)**. It celebrates the diverse beauty of Ireland’s rivers, streams, lakes, and coastal waters.

## Key Dates
* **Closing Date:** Friday, 22nd May 2026
* **Closing Time:** 5:00 PM IST
* **Photo Eligibility:** Images must have been taken on the island of Ireland within the last six months (since late November 2025).

## Submission Details
* **Platform:** Entries must be submitted via the official [LAWPRO Submission Portal](https://consult.watersandcommunities.ie/en/content/my-favourite-waterbody-photo-competition-2026).
* **File Formats:** JPEG, PNG, or TIF.
* **Max File Size:** 15 MB.
* **Required Info:** A brief description (a few lines) explaining why the chosen waterbody is important to you.

## Categories & Perspectives
The competition is broad and inclusive of various photographic styles:
* **Coastal & Marine:** Including shots from the Atlantic coast and offshore islands.
* **Inland Waters:** Rivers, streams, and loughs.
* **Underwater Photography:** Fully eligible as long as the shot is within Irish marine or freshwater territory.
* **Note:** It is recommended to avoid photos containing recognizable individuals.

## Prizes
* **Overall Winner:** A €550 voucher for Sheen Falls Lodge in Kenmare, Co. Kerry, plus an A3 framed print of the winning entry.
* **Recognition:** Winning photographs will be featured in the official **2027 LAWPRO Calendar**.

## Tips for a Strong Entry
* **Unique Perspective:** Consider using specialized techniques like macro or wide-angle underwater photography to stand out from standard landscape entries.
* **Technical Quality:** Ensure sharp focus and proper exposure. For underwater shots, careful lighting (using strobes or external triggers) can help reveal the vibrant colors of local biodiversity.
* **Storytelling:** Use the required description to highlight the ecological or personal significance of the location.

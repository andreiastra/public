# Nauticam Enhanced Viewfinder Summary for OM-1 Mark II

This document summarizes the research and technical details regarding the installation of an enhanced viewfinder for the **Nauticam NA-OM1** housing, specifically focused on improving underwater macro photography.

## 1. Primary Recommendation: The 45° Viewfinder
For macro photography, the **45° Enhanced Viewfinder** is considered the superior investment over the 180° version. It allows for a more comfortable diving posture when shooting subjects close to the seabed.

* **Model:** Nauticam 45° Enhanced Viewfinder
* **SKU:** 32213 (Full Frame/DSLR version)
* **Optical Specs:** 32° Internal Angle / 1:1 Magnification.
* **Benefit:** Provides a 1:1 un-vignetted image with a larger, clearer view of the camera's electronic viewfinder (EVF). This is critical for nailing focus on tiny subjects when using the 60mm Macro and CMC-1.

## 2. Why the "Full Frame/DSLR" Version (SKU 32213)?
Although the OM-1 Mark II is a mirrorless system, the Full Frame version is preferred for several technical reasons:

* **Superior Eye Relief:** The larger glass elements mean you don't have to press your diving mask as tightly against the viewfinder to see the entire frame.
* **Optical Clarity:** It offers a brighter and more immersive view of the high-resolution EVF.
* **Versatility:** By using the 32213 with an adapter, you own the highest-quality optic Nauticam produces, compatible with future housing upgrades.

## 3. Benefits for Short-Sighted Users (-1.0 Prescription)
The 32213 is highly beneficial for users with mild short-sightedness:
* **External Diopter Adjustment:** Features a large, accessible knob to adjust the focus of the viewfinder image.
* **Prescription Compensation:** With a -1.0 prescription, the diopter range (typically -3 to +3) allows for a perfectly sharp image without needing prescription mask lenses or contacts.
* **Reduced Eye Strain:** Eliminates the "dead zone" distance of the rear LCD, providing a clear, projected image that reduces fatigue during long dives.

## 4. Compatibility & "MIL" Context
The NA-OM1 is a **MIL (Mirrorless Interchangeable Lens)** housing. 
* **Required Adapter:** To mount the larger 32213 viewfinder, you must use **Adapter 32215**. This kit provides the necessary collar to bridge the viewfinder to the mirrorless housing.

## 5. Technical Installation Details
Installation involves replacing the housing's standard eyepiece with the viewfinder collar.

### Key Components for Installation:
* **Viewfinder Collar:** Part of the 32215 adapter kit.
* **Installation Tool:** A Nauticam spanner tool (required to unscrew the internal retaining ring).
* **O-ring Maintenance:** Essential cleaning and lubrication of the O-ring (using Nauticam blue grease) to maintain waterproof integrity. 
* **Post-Install Check:** A vacuum test (using the M14 Valve II) is mandatory before the first dive.

## 🔍 The "Nudi-Sniper" Logic (C1 Profile)
You are currently using Custom Mode 1 (C1) for macro work with a focus on precision S-AF (Single AF).

### 1. The Subject Detection "Hack"
Despite no "Fish" mode, the following is the forum-proven "meta":
* Mode: Bird Detection
* Why: AI identifies rhinophores (horns) as eyes/heads.
* Activation: Assigned to a physical button (ISO or AEL/AFL) for instant toggling.

### 2. AF Scanner (The Hunting Fix)
* Location: `Menu > 4. AF > AF Scanner`
* Setting: OFF
* Benefit: Prevents the 60mm lens from racking to infinity if it loses focus. This keeps the subject "visible" in the EVF even during a focus miss, allowing for rapid recovery.

### 3. Lens Hardware Limiter
* Switch Position: 0.19m - 0.4m
* Result: Physically restricts the lens motor to the macro range, significantly increasing AF lock speed.

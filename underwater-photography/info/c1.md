# Custom Mode C1: Underwater Macro Settings

## Lens

* M.Zuiko 60mm Macro
* Physical Switch: 0.19m - ∞ (Must be set to FULL to enable internal AF Limiter)

## Exposure

* Mode: Manual (M)
* Aperture: f/10 – f/14 (**The "Secret Handshake" Strategy**)
    * *f/10:* Peak sharpness for stationary subjects.
    * *f/14:* Optimal compromise for M43; 50% more DOF than f/9 while avoiding the diffraction "mush" of f/20.
* Shutter Speed: 1/250s (Sync limit; essential for killing ambient green water)
* ISO: 200 (Drop to ISO 80 for pitch-black backgrounds)

## Focus

* AF Mode: S-AF
* AF Illuminator: OFF
* AF Target: Small point
* AF+MF: OFF
    * Not using Nauticam O60-F Focus Gear for now
* Back Button Focus: ON (Assigned to AF-ON)
* Shutter Half-press: AF On
* AF Limiter: OFF (Preset: On1)
    * Does not work well. Need to play with it
* Distance for On1: 0.1m – 0.4m
    * Covers subjects from port glass to ~0.5m physical distance.*
* AF Scanner: OFF (Prevents lens from "hunting" to infinity if AF misses)
* Release Priority S-AF: OFF
    * Need to investigate more
* Subject Detection: ON/OFF (Cats&Dogs AI)
   * the Animal AI on the Mark II has been specifically refined to recognize a broader range of silhouettes that more closely match fish, nudibranchs, and other marine life.
    * Set dedicated button ISO

## Customized function for Fn lever

1. FNo, Shutter
2. Flash Exposure, ISO

## Focus Aids

* Focus Peaking: OFF (Can be toggled ON for high-contrast night subjects)
* MF Assist / Magnify: OFF (Map 'Magnify' to a housing button for manual verification)

## Flash / Strobe

* Trigger: Backscatter OM Smart Trigger
* Camera Menu: Flash Fill-In (RC Mode ON)
* Strobe: Backscatter Hybrid Flash (HF-1)
* Strobe Dial: **SC Macro** (Smart Control Macro)
* Flash Mode: TTL / SC Macro 
    * *Note:* Use **Flash Comp -0.7 to -1.0** to prevent "blooming" on white nudibranchs or reflective squid skin.
* Diffuser: Flat White Diffuser (Expands beam to 140°)
* LED Light Mode: Wide
* LED Intensity: High (Essential for AF contrast at f/14)

## Metering

* Picture Mode: Natural
* Metering: Spot

## Drive / Stabilization

* Drive Mode: Single Shot
* Image Stabilization: S-IS1
